About

The gap between foundation models and real decisions is architecture. Not prompts. Not wrappers. Prediction layers over 122M+ indexed compounds, compliance systems that understand jurisdictional complexity, orchestration that gives models the specialized tools they need to move from conversation to computation.

NovoQuantNexus builds computational infrastructure for drug discovery and materials science. Two MCP servers — NovoMCP for discovery intelligence and Novo Compute for GPU simulation — expose 62 tools to any AI assistant. From target identification to patient stratification. From OLED screening to electrolyte stability. One platform, two verticals.

NovoWorkbench is the desktop surface. Researchers paste a molecule and get 3D structure, pharmacokinetic predictions, regulatory screening, and AI-optimized analogs — in under 2 seconds. Model-agnostic. Local-first. Signed and notarized by Apple.

Before this: autonomous discovery systems in high-stakes manufacturing — reinforcement learning agents that diagnosed failures and improved without intervention. The work is AI infrastructure. The domain is wherever the stakes are high enough to demand it.

    Molecular Targets Shape Reasoning Topology: Bidirectional Validation of Chemistry-Inspired Bond Classification for AI Reasoning Chains
    ChemRxiv Preprint
    Key Finding: Molecular targets determine AI reasoning topology with large effect sizes (Cohen's d = 0.94 and 1.75, both p < 10−9). Across 478 reasoning traces from five ADMET endpoints, CYP2D6 reasoning shows significantly more covalent (deductive) bonds while hERG reasoning shows more van der Waals (exploratory) bonds — closing a bidirectional loop where chemistry metaphors explain reasoning structure, and chemistry problems shape it.

    NovoExpert: State-of-the-Art ADMET Prediction on TDC Benchmarks
    ChemRxiv Preprint
    Key Finding: Five SOTA wins on Therapeutics Data Commons ADMET endpoints — CYP2D6 (0.778 AUPRC), CYP3A4 (0.916 AUPRC), CYP3A4 Substrate (0.648 AUPRC), Clearance Hepatocyte (0.602 Spearman), and DILI (0.922 AUROC). CatBoost ensembles with MapLight (2573-bit) and GIN (300-dim) fingerprints, supplemented by Chemprop v2 D-MPNN for DILI.

Systems · Regulatory
FAVES V4 — Compliance Inline
Compliance at the end of a pipeline is a gate. Compliance inside the pipeline is infrastructure. FAVES V4 is a three-tier detection system — whitelist matching, direct name resolution, scaffold analysis with 1,585 structural alert patterns — screened against 8 jurisdictions before a molecule reaches your team. BOILED-Egg permeability. Prior art checks. 100% sensitivity and specificity on a 102-compound benchmark.
