About

I build intelligence infrastructure—the systems between foundation models and real decisions.

AI reasons, but it doesn't act. The gap is architecture: prediction layers over massive datasets, compliance systems that adapt to jurisdictional complexity, and orchestration that gives models the specialized tools they need to move from conversation to computation.

I close that gap. I specialize in moving AI from "demo" to "production" by building the systems that handle failure, compliance, and scale automatically.

My work establishes the blueprint for high-stakes intelligence—100M+ records indexed with pre-computed ML predictions, regulatory compliance across 8 jurisdictions, and production MCP infrastructure running natively in the Anthropic ecosystem. The architecture translates: these patterns are essential wherever precision meets scale.
